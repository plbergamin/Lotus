Our story...

At Lotus Studio, our story begins with a passion for holistic wellness and a desire to create a sanctuary for those seeking balance in their lives. Founded by dedicated yoga enthusiasts, our studio is more than just a place to practice; it’s a community where individuals come together to explore the depths of their physical and spiritual well-being. From our humble beginnings, we've grown into a vibrant space where every class is designed to nurture, heal, and inspire. Join us, and become a part of our journey towards a healthier, more harmonious life.
